About Us

Final Escort is the debut game produced by the Game Maker's Union, a student-led game design studio at Toronto Metropolitan University. Over the academic year, students across diverse programs at the university will work together in a one-of-its-kind interdisciplinary learning and building exercise to conceptualize, design, and produce the game. Our ultimate goal is to take the project to the market by the academic year's end to showcase the student's innovation and creativity.
