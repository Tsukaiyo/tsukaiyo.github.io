When the royal family falls to an uprising, a lone Knight flees with the Princess. Her life and the kingdom's future are in your hands. With 10 days to navigate treacherous paths and lurking dangers, every decision you make will shape its fate. How far will loyalty take you when the kingdom's future is hanging by a thread?

Releasing April 2025


Departments

Accessibility

Making sure the game is playable for everyone, with features that support a wide range of abilities.

Art

Bringing the characters and story world to life through our visuals, allowing the players to immerse themselves into a vivid game play experience.


Game Design

Conceptualizing and designing an engaging gameplay to keep players hooked from start to finish.

Programming

Building the game.


Quality Assurance

Ensuring the game is clear, fun, and bug-free.

Sound Design

Composing and recording original music and sound effects, to enhance the gameplay experience with immersive audio.


User Interface (UI)

Designing easy-to-use interfaces and experiences that make playing the game enjoyable and intuitive.

Writing

Crafting the interactive story, dialogue and building engaging lore that will allow players to shape the game's narrative through their choices.
