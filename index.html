When the royal family falls to an uprising, a lone Knight flees with the Princess. Her life and the kingdom's future are in your hands. With only days to navigate treacherous paths and lurking dangers, every decision you make will shape its fate. How far will loyalty take you when the kingdom's future is hanging by a thread?
