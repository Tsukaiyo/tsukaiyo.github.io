The QA Team

Eamon Boily, QA Lead

I love making games both physical and digital. One of my biggest interests in game making is creating simple games that still allow for depth and complexity. In QA, our goal is to take the first iterations of a game and polish it until it becomes a finished product. We achieve this goal through rigorous play-testing and by working close with the other development teams. QA is a vital role in any game development studio and can make or break the user experience.

What do we do?

Document Bugs
Break the Game
Ensure Fun Gameplay
Maintain Quality Standards
